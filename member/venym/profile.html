Venym

Is one of the two owners of the Minelife SMP and has been a member since the start of Season I.

In Season I, he built a farming base and created a big shop called "Punkt Corp" with Craemon. They began to construct a big harbor on a mushroom island which was never finished. Venym also participated in Jo_0924's PVP Event which he won. He also built the town hall, together with Jo_0924, and became mayor of the server. At the end of Season I, he built his own base, where he built a few houses in a birch forest, all connected by a road.

In Season II he started alone and wanted to build a big tower in the middle of the desert which he never finished. He also built an ugly shop in the shopping district and started to build a PVP Arena with Craemon.

At the beginning of Season III Venym built his starter base, a valley in the jungle near the spawn, with Craemon once again. His plan for his base is a big castle. Venym organized the Season III PVP Event and lost in the final against Lukecraft. At Christmas, he built a giant gingerbread house.

Season III Addons

The Minelife SMP is a vanilla+ server, it runs on Fabric and has a few modifications to the game but remains in the normal Minecraft style.
Most custom content runs purely server-side with a few optional client mods for a nicer experience. This allows even players with unmodified games to join.
In this article you will find everything about our addons and custom content in Season III!
